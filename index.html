About

A bit about me

Photo

I'm originally from India, and I've been living and studying in Riga, Latvia for a few years now. I'm in my third year of a Bachelor's in International Tourism and Hospitality Management at Turiba University.

Right now I work as a Commis I / Kitchen Assistant at Pavāru Māja, a Michelin Green Star kitchen in Līgatne. Before that, I worked as a bartender and did an F&B internship at a 5-star hotel in Greece.

Outside of the kitchen and classroom, I spend time building small AI agent projects, exploring crypto and stock trading, and thinking about how hospitality and technology can work together.
